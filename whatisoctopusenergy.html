Everything You Need to Know About Octopus Energy

When was Octopus Energy founded?

Octopus Energy was founded in August 2015 and began trading in December 2015 by its parent company, Octopus Capital Limited. Although relatively new compared to the big six, the company has seen rapid growth.

Where is Octopus Energy based?

Octopus Energy is headquartered in London and has offices in Warwick, Brighton and Leicester in the UK, as well as in Australia, the USA and Germany.

How has Octopus Energy acquired its customers?

Octopus Energy has acquired customers by purchasing other energy companies, inheriting customers from other energy companies that have ceased trading, and replacing other energy companies as the energy supplier for certain brands such as M&S and Coop. This has allowed Octopus to grow quickly and reach over 1 million UK customers.

Octopus Energy has had an energy procurement deal with Shell, inherited customers from Iresa Limited after it ceased trading, replaced SSE as M&S energy supplier, and purchased Affect Energy in 2018. In 2019 Octopus entered into an agreement with Coop resulting in the gain of over 300,000 customers, and purchased the GB Energy and Flow Energy brands. In 2020, Octopus launched London Power, purchased Engie UK, and Upside Energy.

What energy companies has Octopus Energy acquired?

Octopus Energy purchased Affect Energy in 2018, GB Energy and Flow Energy in 2019, and ENGIE UK and Upside Energy in 2020.

What markets does Octopus Energy serve?

Octopus Energy provides energy services in the UK and Germany.

Is Octopus Energy a unicorn?

A unicorn is a tech start-up valued at over £1 Billion. Octopus Energy became a unicorn in 2020 and achieved unicorn status thanks to an investment of £270 million from Australia’s Origin Energy. Currently, it has achieved double unicorn status thanks to a strategic partnership with Tokyo Gas.

Who is the CEO of Octopus Energy?

Octopus Energy was founded by Greg Jackson and Pete Miller in 2015. Greg Jackson currently holds the position of CEO.

What is Octopus Energy’s mission statement?

“We’re doing energy better — for you and for the environment.” – Source.

How green is Octopus Energy?

Octopus Energy invests heavily in solar energy through its parent and sister companies Octopus Group and Octopus Renewables. It supports a range of renewable projects, encourages households and businesses to generate and sell energy back to the grid, invests in cutting-edge low-carbon technologies, and has started the Future Energy Research Centre. The company is also working to counteract worldwide carbon emissions by planting trees and has special tariffs designed for electric car owners.

Is Octopus Energy good value for Money?

Whilst energy prices fluctuate and different companies have different promotions and offers at different times, Octopus Energy has repeatedly won the Which? Recommended Provider status, suggesting it is good value for money. Octopus is incredibly transparent with their pricing

Does Octopus Energy supply gas?

Yes. Although Octopus has aspirations of focusing on a 100% renewable electric smart grid, the reality is that many households still need gas, so Octopus supplies it.

Does Octopus Energy supply electricity?

Yes. The company’s main focus is to generate and provide 100% renewable electricity.
